Computes the determinant of a 2x2 matrix stored in T, Z, Y, X.

Column 1 | Column 2
---------|---------
T | Y
Z | X

The result will be found in the X register.

Example

1 <ENTER> 2 <ENTER> 3 <ENTER> 4
 
Column 1 | Column 2
---------|---------
1 | 3
2 | 4

XEQ \[LABEL\]

Column 1 | Column 2
---------|---------
* | *
* |-2
